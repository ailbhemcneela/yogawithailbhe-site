Classes & events

Dear Me

An evening to come back to yourself. Slow yoga, a letter written to yourself after an hour on the mat, and yoga nidra to close. Ten people, candles lit, everything provided. Runs a few times a year.

Into the Flow

A four-week series for beginners or those coming back to the mat after a break. The setup of a normal studio can sometimes be overwhelming when you're starting from scratch — here we move together step by step, building your confidence to attend any studio class. Each week builds on the last until by week four, it flows. Small group, six people maximum, all equipment provided.

Slow Flow

A weekly drop-in class for all levels. Hatha-based, gentle, with breathwork and a proper rest at the end. This isn't about getting it right. It's about showing up.

Slow Burn

A candlelit evening of slow yoga and meditation. We'll work with an ancient candle gazing practice that's surprisingly simple and calming — the kind of thing you'll find yourself coming back to on a weeknight evening when you need to reset.
